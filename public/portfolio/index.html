Hi there! You can call me Enya. I completed my double-major undergraduate in Computer Science and Digital Media Studies at University of Rochester, and earned my master degree in Information Science with a UX design concentration at Cornell University.

After graduation, I joined BMW China R&D Center as a UX Prototyper, focusing on designing and building high-fidelity interactive prototypes for mass-production and innovative HMI projects. I'm constantly exploring emerging tech trends, especially AI and intelligent interaction, and I love translating these cutting-edge insights into tangible, user-centered design solutions.

I'm self-motivated and always open to new challenges. What drives me most is solving real-world problems with creativity and rigorous design thinking.
