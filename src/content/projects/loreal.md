    title: "Brandstorm 2022 - Smart Instructional Mirror"
    text: "This is our team design for L'Oréal 2022 BRANSTORM Competition. We noticed that with the increasing diversification in cosmetic product categories and sophistication in makeup procedures, people usually face difficulties in obtaining makeup techniques. With the idea that everyone deserves beauty, we believe this user pain point should be addressed to allow people to try out different styles of beauty. Many beginners are confused with the order to apply different cosmetic products, feel difficult to follow video or text tutorials. Also, the video tutorials cannot cover all of the techniques that tailor to a person’s specific facial features such as eye shape and skin conditions. Even for people who already have some experience with doing makeup, they sometimes complain that it’s difficult for them to try a different style other than routine ones. To improve the makeup experience, our solution is a smart first-person-view instructional mirror that provides hand-on makeup assistance. Users can customize their styles and apply by simply following the guidelines. As a result, we want to make makeup as easy as a coloring game! In this project, I brainstormed and disscussed with my teammates, and designed the conceptual prototype with Figma."
